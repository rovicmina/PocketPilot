## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Login
**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Open the app and tap "Create Account". Enter your email address and create a password. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account.

**Q: I forgot my password. How can I reset it?**
A: On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. You'll receive instructions to reset your password.

**Q: Can I use the app without creating an account?**
A: No, an account is required to save your financial data securely in the cloud.

### Budgeting Features
**Q: How do I create a budget?**
A: Go to the "Budgets" section and tap the "+" button. Enter a name for your budget, select a category, set your spending limit, and choose the time period.

**Q: Can I edit or delete a budget?**
A: Yes, swipe left on any budget in the list to edit or delete it.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my budget?**
A: You'll receive a notification alerting you that you've exceeded your budget limit. The app will also show your budget in red to indicate overspending.

### Expense Tracking
**Q: How do I add an expense?**
A: Tap the "+" button on the main screen or in the "Expenses" section. Enter the amount, select a category, add a description, and choose the date.

**Q: Can I add recurring expenses?**
A: Yes, when adding an expense, you can set it to repeat daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly.

**Q: How do I view my spending history?**
A: Go to the "Expenses" section to see a chronological list of all your expenses. You can also view charts in the "Insights" section.

### Data & Privacy
**Q: Is my financial data secure?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use Firebase security protocols to protect your information.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Currently, you can view and manage your data within the app. Export functionality will be added in a future update.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.

### Technical Issues
**Q: The app is crashing. What should I do?**
A: Try force closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, please contact support with details about your device and when the crashes occur.

**Q: I'm not receiving notifications. How can I fix this?**
A: Check your device's notification settings to ensure PocketPilot is allowed to send notifications. Also verify that notification settings are enabled within the app.

**Q: The app is using too much battery.**
A: The app is designed to be battery-efficient. If you're experiencing issues, try clearing the app cache in your device settings.

## Troubleshooting Guide

### Common Issues and Solutions

**Issue: Unable to login**
- Solution: Check your internet connection, verify your email and password, ensure your account is verified

**Issue: Data not syncing**
- Solution: Check internet connection, force close and reopen the app, verify you're logged in

**Issue: Budget notifications not working**
- Solution: Check device notification settings, verify notification permissions for the app, check app notification settings

**Issue: App running slowly**
- Solution: Clear app cache, restart device, ensure sufficient storage space available

## Data Deletion Process
To permanently delete your account and all associated data:
1. Open the app and go to Settings
2. Select "Account"
3. Tap "Delete Account"
4. Confirm your decision
5. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
