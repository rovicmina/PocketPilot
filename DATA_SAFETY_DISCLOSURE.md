# Data Safety Disclosure for PocketPilot

## Data Collection and Usage

### Data We Collect

**Personal Information (Optional)**
- Email address (for account creation and authentication)
- Profile information (profession, age, gender) if voluntarily provided

**Financial Information (Optional)**
- Budget data entered by the user
- Expense records created by the user
- Income information provided by the user

**Device Information (Automatically Collected)**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App usage statistics
- Crash reports and performance data

### How We Use Your Data

**Providing and Improving Our Service**
- To create and maintain your account
- To process and display your financial information
- To personalize your experience
- To improve app functionality and performance
- To troubleshoot technical issues

**Communication**
- To send you important updates about the app
- To respond to your support requests

### Data Sharing

**Third-Party Services**
We use Firebase services for:
- Authentication
- Data storage
- Analytics (to improve our service)

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect anonymous usage data.

**No Sale of Personal Information**
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### Data Security

**Protection Measures**
- All data is transmitted using secure encryption (HTTPS)
- User authentication is handled through secure Firebase Authentication
- Data is stored in secure Firebase databases
- Regular security audits and updates

### Data Retention and Deletion

**Retention**
We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services.

**Deletion**
You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through:
1. The app settings (if available)
2. Contacting our support team at support@pocketpilot.app

Upon request, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

### Children's Privacy

Our app is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such collection, we will promptly delete the information.

### International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, including the United States where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

### Changes to This Disclosure

We may update this data safety disclosure from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new disclosure on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

### Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our data practices, please contact us at:
- Email: support@pocketpilot.app
